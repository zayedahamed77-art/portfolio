PROJECT

Farmlink


Farmlink is a web-based platform that connects farmers directly with customers. Farmers can upload and sell their agricultural products online, while customers can browse products, place orders, and buy directly from farmers without middlemen. This helps farmers earn better profits and allows customers to get fresh products at fair prices. The system also includes an admin panel to manage users, products, orders, and complaints. It is developed using PHP, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and SQLite.
